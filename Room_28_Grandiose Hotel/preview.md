**Grandiose Hotel Web Application Lab Preview**

Welcome to the Grandiose Hotel web application lab hosted in a secure cloud environment. This application allows you to explore the hotel's online booking system, view contact information, and navigate through the main pages without any setup required on your part.

**Getting Started:**
- **Home Page:** Explore the welcome page to familiarize yourself with the hotel's offerings.
- **Booking Page:** Submit your booking details using the provided XML form to test the application's data handling.
- **Contact Page:** Access contact information for further inquiries.

Begin your assessment by navigating to the **Booking** section and interacting with the booking form to uncover potential areas of interest.

Enjoy your testing!
